Helpful tips for Eczema

Coconut Oil

Coconut oil has antibacterial, anti-fungal, antioxidant and antimicrobial properties that can help soothe your skin when it's itchy during a flare-up.

Humidifier

Humidifiers can help alleviate the symptoms of dry eyes, eczema, itchy skin, cracked lips, and nose bleeds associated with asthma

Vitamins & supplements

Vitamins & supplements can strengthen skin barriers. Some vitamins have fat-soluble antioxidant that help protect skin cells.

Epsom Salt Baths

Epsom salt to the bath water can help ease the discomfort, decrease the itching, inflammation, and redness eczema can cause.
